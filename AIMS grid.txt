Sales Insights Data Analysis






AIMS grid :is project management tool it have  4 components


1)purpose - To unlock sales insights that are not visible before for sales team for taking decisions and automate them to reduce manual time spent on data gathering
2)stakeholders - IT, Sales team, marketing team ,sales director and data analytics team
3)End result - an automated dashboard that providing quick and latest sales insights in order to support data-driven decision making
4)Success criteria -
• Dashboard(s) uncovering sales order insights with latest data available

• Sales team able to take better decisions & prove 10% cost savings of total spending 

• Sales Analysts stop data gathering manually in order to save 20% of their business time and reinvest it value added activity
